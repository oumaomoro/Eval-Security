# 📂 Cyber Insurance Portfolio Guide

Master your organization's cyber insurance posture with Costloci's AI-driven extraction and comparison engine.

## 📥 Policy Ingestion
Upload your `.pdf` or `.docx` policy documents. The AI engine performs a deep multi-track analysis to extract:
1. **Financial Limits**: Annual aggregate, per-occurrence, and critical sub-limits.
2. **Operations**: Waiting periods for Business Interruption and System Failure.
3. **Legal**: Exclusions, endorsement triggers, and notification timelines.

## ⚖️ Weighted Risk Scoring (40/30/20/10)
Your policy is scored on a scale of 1-100 based on the following professional weighting:
- **40% Exclusions**: Quantity and severity of coverage carve-outs.
- **30% Limits/Deductibles**: Alignment of sub-limits with your aggregate exposure.
- **20% Waiting Periods**: Operational impact of BI/System downtime triggers.
- **10% Notifications**: Clarity and speed of required breach reporting.

## 🔍 Side-by-Side Comparison
When comparing two policies (e.g., during a renewal cycle):
1. Use the **Comparison Hub**.
2. Review the **Delta Analysis** summary.
3. Use the **Diff Viewer** to identify exactly where coverage limits or exclusions have shifted.

## 🖋️ Clause Redlining
If a policy clause is non-compliant or too restrictive:
- Use the **Redline Studio**.
- Select the problematic clause.
- Choose "Suggest Better Language" to align with your organization's legal standards.
