# 🛡️ DPO Command Center Guide

Welcome to the Costloci DPO Dashboard. This guide explains how to leverage the platform's multi-standard compliance and risk management features.

## 📍 Compliance Heatmap (Radar)
The Radar chart provides a high-level view of your organization's alignment with international and local standards:
- **Standards Covered**: KDPA (Kenya), GDPR (EU), CBK (Finance), SOC2, and ISO27001.
- **Interpretation**: A higher radius indicates better alignment. Areas with "Dips" represent jurisdictional drift or missing clauses in your contract portfolio.

## ⚡ Breach Notification Readiness
The Pie chart (Barometer) represents your estimated capability to respond to a data breach within regulatory windows (e.g., 72 hours for GDPR/KDPA):
- **Target**: 82%+ is considered "Optimized".
- **Calculated By**: AI audits of DPA (Data Processing Addendum) clauses, existence of response playbooks, and jurisdictional mapping.

## 🛠️ Remediation Workflow
1. **Identify**: Review "Critical Findings" on the DPO dashboard.
2. **Fix**: Click "Fix Clause" to enter the Redlining Studio.
3. **Execute**: Use the AI Redliner to suggest compliant language from the **Clause Library**.
4. **Rescan**: Trigger a "Jurisdictional Sweep" to update your scores after contract updates.

---
**DPO Tip**: Use the **Strategic Pack** (under Regulatory Alerts) to export all evidence for external audits or board reporting.
