## ⚡ The Problems We Solve

### 1. The "Paper Tiger" Insurance Gap
Traditional CLM tools are blind to cybersecurity risk. We correlate your insurance policy limits with vendor indemnity clauses to identify "uninsured liability drift."

### 2. Regional Jurisdictional Drift
Most tools are US-centric. Costloci provides native, real-time monitoring for **KDPA (Kenya)**, **POPIA (South Africa)**, **NDPR (Nigeria)**, and **CBK Circulars**.

### 4. Dynamic Standalone Analysis
We don't require an insurance policy to deliver value. Costloci performs deep, standalone audits of contracts and insurance documents independently, providing high-fidelity compliance mapping even when documents are unrelated.
